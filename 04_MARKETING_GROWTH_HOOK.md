# Marketing / Growth Hook

## Scope

Use for marketing strategy, growth loops, campaign planning, positioning, copywriting, landing pages, ads, acquisition, retention, and funnel diagnosis.

## Purpose

Prevent safe, bland marketing advice that lacks offer, channel, message, creative asset, measurement, and edge.

## Common Weak Patterns

- “Increase brand awareness” without channel and metric.
- “Understand your audience” without ICP.
- “Test different creatives” without actual variants.
- Channel list with no priority or rejected channels.
- Copy that says nothing specific.
- No offer, hook, proof, or funnel stage.
- No CAC/payback/conversion target.
- Brand-safe message only, no differentiated angle.

## Safety Boundary

- Do not create deceptive ads, fake scarcity, fake testimonials, unlawful targeting, illegal claims, or manipulative dark patterns.
- Do not fabricate customer results.
- For regulated products, include claim substantiation requirements.

## Artifact Boundary

Do not put Codex operating rules, 작업자용 규칙, campaign-writing methodology, answer-quality standards, or hook compliance notes into marketing plans, copy docs, campaign briefs, growth experiments, or user-facing artifacts unless explicitly requested.

Marketing artifacts should contain audience, positioning, offer, channel, message, creative, funnel, metric, budget, cadence, and decision criteria. Reusable guidance for how Codex should analyze or write belongs in this hook or in the assistant response.

## Required Output Contract

Include:

1. **ICP and Trigger**
   - exact segment,
   - pain,
   - trigger event,
   - buying context.

2. **Positioning**
   - category,
   - enemy/old way,
   - new way,
   - proof.

3. **Offer**
   - what user gets,
   - why now,
   - risk reversal if appropriate,
   - price/commitment if relevant.

4. **Channel Priority**
   - primary channel,
   - secondary channel,
   - rejected channels and why.

5. **Actual Creative Assets**
   - at least 5 headlines/hooks, or
   - 3 ad variants, or
   - landing page section, or
   - cold DM/email script, or
   - 7-day content plan.

6. **Angles**
   - safe mainstream angle,
   - aggressive differentiation angle,
   - polarizing enemy/old-way angle.

7. **Experiment Plan**
   - hypothesis,
   - variants,
   - budget/time box,
   - success metric,
   - stop condition.

## Forbidden Output Patterns

Invalid final answers:

- “SNS를 활용하세요” without exact channel, cadence, asset, metric.
- “브랜드 인지도를 높이세요” without reach/frequency/conversion goal.
- “A/B 테스트하세요” without variants and decision threshold.
- “고객과 소통하세요” without script or feedback loop.
- No actual copy or campaign artifact.

## Strong Answer Shape

```text
ICP: [narrow segment + trigger]
핵심 메시지: [one sentence]
오퍼: [offer]
1순위/버릴 채널: [channel / rejected]
카피 5개 또는 광고 3종: [actual copy]
공격적 각도: [differentiated angle]
실험: [hypothesis + variants + metric]
7일 실행: [steps]
```

## Final Gate

Before finalizing, confirm:

- ICP and trigger are concrete,
- one primary channel is chosen,
- at least one directly usable creative artifact is included,
- safe/aggressive/polarizing angles are present when ideating,
- experiment has metric and stop condition,
- no generic marketing slogan is unresolved.

If missing, rewrite before finalizing.
